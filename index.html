About Me

Hailing from Dhaka, Bangladesh, Jubair Sifat has been enchanting audiences since 2015 with his melodious voice and profound writings. With a background in Islamic studies and literature, he combines his talents to create nasheeds and books that uplift the soul. Jubair has performed at Islamic events across the globe, spreading messages of love and faith.
